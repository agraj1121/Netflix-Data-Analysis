# Netflix Data Analysis 

## Project Overview

This project explores Netflix's content library using exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques. The goal is to uncover trends in content distribution, growth, genres, and geographical contributions to better understand Netflix's content strategy.

It answers the Umbrella Question - "What kind of content does Netflix invest in and how has it changed over time?"

## Dataset

The dataset was taken from Kaggle.

This dataset contains information about Netflix titles, including:

* Title
* Type (Movie / TV Show)
* Director
* Cast
* Country
* Date Added
* Release Year
* Rating
* Duration
* Genre (Listed_In)
* Description

## Objectives

The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

1. Is Netflix mostly Movies or TV Shows?
2. How did Netflix's content library grow from 2011 to 2020?
3. Which countries contribute the most content to Netflix?
4. Which Directors have the most movies on Netflix?
5. What are the Top 5 Genres available on Netflix?

## Tools & Libraries Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook
* VS Code

## Analysis & Findings

### 1. Movies vs TV Shows

* Movies dominate Netflix's catalog.
* TV Shows represent a smaller but significant portion of the platform's content.

### 2. Content Growth (2011–2020)

* Netflix experienced rapid content expansion over the analyzed period.
* The highest growth occurred in the later years as the platform expanded globally.

### 3. Top Countries on Netflix

* The United States contributed the largest number of titles.
* Countries such as India and the United Kingdom also play a major role in content production.

### 5. Top Genres on Netflix

The most common genres include:

* Drama
* International Movies
* Comedy
* Documentaries
* Action & Adventure

These genres indicate Netflix's focus on diverse and globally appealing content.

## Key Insights

* Movies form the majority of Netflix's content library.
* Content additions increased significantly after 2015.
* The United States remains the dominant content-producing country.
* International content plays an important role in catalog diversity.
* Drama and International Movies are among the most popular genres.
* Netflix's strategy emphasizes global reach, content diversity, and continuous catalog expansion.

## Conclusion

Netflix has evolved into a globally diversified streaming platform with a vast collection of movies and TV shows. The analysis reveals strong growth in content volume, broad international representation, and a wide range of genres designed to serve audiences worldwide.

Overall, Netflix's success is supported by its commitment to expanding content availability, investing in international markets, and maintaining a diverse entertainment portfolio.
